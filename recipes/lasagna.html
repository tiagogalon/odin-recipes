Lasagna

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles. Originally, the word lasagna was used to describe a pot in which food was being cooked rather than the pasta itself. Lasagna recipes have been around for ages, dating back to as early as the thirteenth century.Today, lasagnas come in all kinds of varieties from veggies and cream sauces to traditional sauce and cheese lasagnas as well as meat lasagnas.Originated in Italy during the middle ages, lasagna has been attributed to the city of Naples.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausag and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You will need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Tow tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley,dried basil leaves,salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it does not ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it

Step-by-step of How to make a delicious lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layer, then top with the remaining Parmesan.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes. Also, It is important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
